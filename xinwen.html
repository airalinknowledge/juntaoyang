《轻巧而冷淡的对谈：大西洋，算法与可见性》在Varsity Film Expo获得了荣誉提及。 评审团的颁奖词中表示，该片 "...serves as a powerful commentary on the complex relationship between technology, race, justice, and representation. The film's focus on digital representation and the politics of images connects to the discussions around digital inclusion and access to technology. The film raises questions about the ethical implications of AI and its potential to exacerbate existing inequalities and biases. The film encourages critical thinking about technology and its role in society, prompting viewers to consider the broader society and environmental impacts of technological advancement...." 作为荣誉的一部分，电影同时也被推荐给了联合国教科文组织。
