Juntao Yang is a critic, curator, and artist.

Yang's critical writings and artistic practices focus on the traces of the operations of micro power in the specific field of visual and material culture, the visible or invisible conflicts and violence in daily existence, and the organization and fluid of the proxy of power. Yang is committed to resorting to Speech, sometimes hysterically, counteracts the silent power of ideology and the pervasive political apathy. Yang's current research focuses on planetary cinema and geological thinking, as well as toxicity and catastrophe media. Yang is currently based in New York and Nanjing.
