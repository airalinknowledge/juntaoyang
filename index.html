Juntao Yang is a critic, curator, and artist.

Yang's critical writing and artistic practice focus on the traces of the operations of micro power in the specific field of visual and material culture, the visible or invisible conflicts and violence in daily existence, and the organization and fluid of the proxy of power. Yang is committed to resorting to Speech, sometimes hysterically, counteracts the silent power of ideology and the pervasive political apathy. Yang's current research focuses on exotic imagination in image representation, digital infrastructure, theater architectural ideology, etc.
